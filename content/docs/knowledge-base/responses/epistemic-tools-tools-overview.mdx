Specific tools and platforms in the <EntityLink id="E122">epistemic infrastructure</EntityLink> space:

**Forecasting & Prediction:**
- **<EntityLink id="E286">Squiggle</EntityLink>**: Probabilistic programming language for uncertainty quantification
- **<EntityLink id="E287">SquiggleAI</EntityLink>**: LLM-powered probabilistic model generation
- **<EntityLink id="E200">Metaforecast</EntityLink>**: Cross-platform forecast aggregation

**Benchmarking:**
- **<EntityLink id="E144">ForecastBench</EntityLink>**: Dynamic benchmark for AI forecasting capabilities
- **<EntityLink id="E10">AI Forecasting Benchmark Tournament</EntityLink>**: Human vs AI forecasting competition

**Research Coordination:**
- **<EntityLink id="E379">XPT</EntityLink>**: Structured adversarial collaboration on existential risks

**Verification:**
- **<EntityLink id="E381">X Community Notes</EntityLink>**: Crowdsourced fact-checking at scale

**Knowledge Coordination:**
- **<EntityLink id="E384">Longterm Wiki</EntityLink>**: Strategic intelligence platform for AI safety prioritization
- **<EntityLink id="E382">Stampy / AISafety.info</EntityLink>**: AI safety Q&A wiki with LLM chatbot
- **<EntityLink id="E383">MIT AI Risk Repository</EntityLink>**: Database of 1,700+ categorized AI risks

**AI-Assisted Knowledge & Encyclopedias:**
- **<EntityLink id="E681">AI-Assisted Knowledge Management</EntityLink>**: Tools for LLM-assisted wiki and knowledge base creation (Obsidian+AI, Notion AI, Golden, NotebookLM, RAG frameworks)
- **<EntityLink id="E682">Grokipedia</EntityLink>**: xAI's AI-generated encyclopedia (6M+ articles); case study in scale vs. quality tradeoffs
- **<EntityLink id="E683">Wikipedia and AI Content</EntityLink>**: Wikipedia's policies, WikiProject AI Cleanup, model collapse, and the sustainability challenge

These tools aim to improve decision-making on high-stakes questions, particularly around AI development and existential risk.
