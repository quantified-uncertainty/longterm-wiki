Approaches and methodologies for building epistemic infrastructure:

**Forecasting Approaches:**
- **<EntityLink id="prediction-markets">Prediction Markets</EntityLink>**: Market-based aggregation of probabilistic beliefs
- **<EntityLink id="ai-forecasting">AI-Augmented Forecasting</EntityLink>**: Using AI to enhance prediction capabilities
- **<EntityLink id="hybrid-systems">AI-Human Hybrid Systems</EntityLink>**: Combining human judgment with AI capabilities

**Deliberation & Coordination:**
- **<EntityLink id="deliberation">AI-Assisted Deliberation</EntityLink>**: Technology-mediated group reasoning
- **<EntityLink id="coordination-tech">Coordination Technologies</EntityLink>**: Tools for collective action and decision-making

**Verification & Authentication:**
- **<EntityLink id="content-authentication">Content Authentication</EntityLink>**: Provenance and authenticity verification
- **<EntityLink id="deepfake-detection">Deepfake Detection</EntityLink>**: AI-generated content identification

**<EntityLink id="collective-epistemics-design-sketches">Design Sketches for Collective Epistemics</EntityLink>** (Forethought Foundation, 2025):
- **<EntityLink id="community-notes-for-everything">Community Notes for Everything</EntityLink>**: Cross-platform AI-generated context annotations on misleading content
- **<EntityLink id="rhetoric-highlighting">Rhetoric Highlighting</EntityLink>**: Automated detection of persuasive-but-misleading language and citation misrepresentation
- **<EntityLink id="reliability-tracking">Reliability Tracking</EntityLink>**: Topic-specific track records for public actors' claims, predictions, and promises
- **<EntityLink id="epistemic-virtue-evals">Epistemic Virtue Evals</EntityLink>**: Benchmarks testing AI systems for calibration, sycophancy resistance, and honesty
- **<EntityLink id="provenance-tracing">Provenance Tracing</EntityLink>**: Infrastructure for transparent knowledge genealogy and claim reliability assessment

See also <EntityLink id="epistemic-infrastructure">Epistemic Infrastructure</EntityLink> for the broader context of these approaches.
