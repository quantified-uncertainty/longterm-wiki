## Relationship to Scenarios

Higher AI capabilities primarily increase the probability and severity of **AI Takeover** scenarios:

- **<EntityLink id="E623">Rapid Takeover</EntityLink>**: Requires sufficient capability for decisive action
- **<EntityLink id="E619">Gradual Takeover</EntityLink>**: Enabled by increasing autonomy and generality over time

Capabilities also affect <EntityLink id="E671">Human-Caused Catastrophe</EntityLink> scenarios by enabling more powerful <EntityLink id="E42">Bioweapons</EntityLink>, <EntityLink id="E86">Cyberweapons</EntityLink>, and <EntityLink id="E35">Autonomous Weapons</EntityLink>.
